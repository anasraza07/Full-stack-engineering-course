What is Hub in Computer Network?

HUB eik simple device hai jo multiple computers/devices ko eik network main connect krta hai

Data broadcast krta hai mtlb jb eik device data bhejta hai to hub us data ko sabhi connected devices tk bhej deta hai

Drawback ye hai ke sb devices eik hi data ko dekhte hain, privacy nhi hoti
